Before looking at price tags, ask yourself: **"What will I use this computer for?"**

*   **The Household/Student User:** Need something for web browsing, homework, Netflix, and light Microsoft Office work? Focus on portability and battery life.
*   **The Professional/Creator:** Do you edit photos, render videos, or run heavy software? You'll need a dedicated Graphics Card (GPU) and a powerful multi-core CPU.
*   **The Corporate/Port User:** Working in logistics, shipping, or heavy Excel data management? Reliability, a robust keyboard, and multiple ports for external monitors are vital.
